Who is James?

I grew up in a suburb of Dublin (Ireland) named Kilbarrack. I went to school in North Dublin and attended university at Trinity College Dublin. Graduating in 2015 with a degree in Natural Science (Geology), I then moved to the UK to study for a Masters in Hydrogeology at University of Birmingham. Following the completion of my studies, I moved to Cambridge (UK) to work as a Hydrogeologist with Mott MacDonald Ltd. For three years I worked on a wide range of projects from contamination risk assessments, groundwater models, strategic water company investment planning and took on various project management roles. In my spare time, I taught myself Python with the intention of automating a number of work processes. Computer programming became the most enjoyable part of my job and I begin exploring the language more and taking on personal projects at home (such as creating my own game). Eventually, I decided I wanted more from my job and that a career in software development was for me! I quit my job, moved to London and was fortunate enough to get a place on the _nology Tech Accelerator programme! You can find out more about me on this website. Read more...
