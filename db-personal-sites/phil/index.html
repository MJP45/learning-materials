About Me

Interests

I'm passionate about boxing having boxed (on and off!) for 20 years. I became a boxing coach at University and went on to work as a coach for a boxing charity when I graduated from University.

Background

Education

Having left school with A-Levels in Politics, English Language and Literature and Religious Studies, I went to Leeds University to study English Language & Literature.

Career

IBM

Leaving University I joined IBM's graduate scheme as an "IT Consultant." I stayed with IBM for 4 years, working on projects whereby I supported client applications. Each role required little technical ability, but prioritised client handling soft skills.

Nomura

After leaving IBM, I joined Investment Banking working for Nomura. For 3 years, I worked as an Application Support Specialist for the Equity Derivatives asset class. I worked alongside front office traders in a high pressure environment, ensuring the applications they use to trade and hedge risk were stable whilst investigating any risk or pricing issues.

Deutsche Bank

In 2014, I moved to Deutsche Bank as an Equity Derivatives Risk Application Specialist, continuing in the same asset class I'd previously worked on at Nomura. I've been working over the last year to learn Python in order to automate the application environment.
