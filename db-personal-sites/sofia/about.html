about me.

Welcome to my website!

I am Sofia and to summarise who I am; I am Finnish, an intersectional feminist, a passionate reader and someone who loves to travel.

Although I am from Finland, I have grown-up across the world, having lived in eight countries spanning three continents. For this reason, I find it difficult to solely identify with being Finnish, which is why the question of; "where are you from?" always spurs me into a nervous panic.

Among other things, I enjoy reading loads, listening to hours of podcasts (mainly on politics), and trying out all types of food from around the world.
